The Royal Game of Ur AI Competition

Language: Java   Date: 2018   Contributers: Tim Kaiser

Description

This project is an AI competition between a friend of mine and I, where try to find out who can make the better artificial intelligence. Both AIs compete in multiple rounds of "The Royal Game of Ur", an 5000 years old mesopotamian game. The winner is who wins the most rounds.

My AI, "The King of Ur"

This AI uses a utility function based on 15 weighted factors to find the optimal move. The factor weights were determined by an evolutionary algorithm in a multi-hour test session.

My Contributions

  • Building the basic game framework for the competition
  • Creating the AI "The King of Ur"
